About ProScholarTools

ProScholarTools is a free AI-powered academic and legal research platform serving PhD researchers, law students, attorneys, and scholars worldwide. We believe world-class research tools should be accessible to everyone — regardless of institution, location, or budget.

🎯 Our Mission

In 2025, the best citation generators are locked behind paywalls. Premium legal research tools cost hundreds of dollars monthly. PhD researchers at underfunded institutions and solo legal practitioners deserve the same quality tools as those at Harvard or elite law firms.

ProScholarTools was built to change that. We offer 22 AI-powered research tools — completely free — covering everything from APA 7th edition citations to IRAC legal brief generation to PhD dissertation planning. Every tool is powered by Claude AI by Anthropic, recognized for exceptional accuracy in academic and legal contexts.

🤖 Our Technology

All 22 tools are powered by Claude AI (claude-sonnet-4-20250514) by Anthropic — widely recognized as one of the most accurate AI models available for nuanced academic and legal content generation.

Unlike rule-based citation generators that apply rigid templates, Claude AI understands context. It correctly handles unusual source types, catches common formatting errors, and can explain its output so you learn while you work.

Privacy First

Your Anthropic API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage. We never see it, store it on our servers, or transmit it anywhere except directly to Anthropic's API from your browser. No account required. No data collection of your research content.

📋 Editorial Standards

ProScholarTools maintains strict editorial standards for all published guides and articles:

  • All citation guides are verified against the latest official style manuals (APA 7th Edition Publication Manual, MLA Handbook 9th Edition, Chicago Manual of Style 17th Edition, IEEE Editorial Style Manual)
  • Legal guides are reviewed for accuracy against current US, UK, and international legal standards
  • All content is updated when official style guides release new editions
  • AI-generated content is reviewed and verified before publication
⚠️ Important Disclaimer

While we strive for accuracy, always verify citations against official style guides and consult qualified professionals for legal advice. AI tools assist — they do not replace expert judgment.
