📖 Which Citation Style Do I Use? — Quick Reference

APA 7th Edition: Psychology, social sciences, education, business, nursing, communication

MLA 9th Edition: English literature, languages, film studies, cultural studies, comparative literature

Chicago 17th Edition: History, political science, theology, fine arts, philosophy, law

IEEE: Electrical engineering, computer science, electronics, telecommunications, information technology

Harvard: Medicine, natural sciences, business (UK/Australian universities), health sciences
