OUR MISSION · مهمتنا

Making Expert-Level Academic Tools Free for Everyone

ProScholarsTools was founded on a simple belief: quality academic tools should not be locked behind expensive subscriptions. Students at underfunded universities, researchers in developing countries, and independent scholars deserve the same quality of support as those at well-resourced institutions.

Every tool on this platform was designed by subject-matter experts — people who have personally struggled with citation formatting at 2 AM, who have written hundreds of legal briefs, who have reviewed thousands of student theses. We built what we always wished existed.

OUR STORY · قصتنا

Welcome to ProScholarsTools

Welcome to ProScholarsTools, the premier AI-powered digital platform specifically designed to elevate the quality of academic research and streamline the workflow of researchers and legal professionals worldwide.

Our Story

The platform was founded by an elite group of university professors and specialists in academic research, law, and artificial intelligence development. Through their extensive experience in academic defense committees and analyzing legal precedents, they recognized the immense challenges and time consumed by summarizing, paraphrasing, and accurate citation formatting.

This platform was built as a practical solution that merges the rigor of academic and legal methodologies with the speed and power of generative AI — powered by Claude AI by Anthropic.

Our Vision

To be the ultimate digital assistant for every researcher, empowering them to produce rigorous, innovative academic work in record time and to the highest global quality standards.
