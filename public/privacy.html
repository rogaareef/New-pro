2. API Keys

If you choose to add your own Anthropic API key for unlimited access, it is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your API key is never transmitted to our servers in stored form
  • We have zero ability to access your API key
  • API requests routed through our proxy do not log your key
  • Clearing your browser data immediately removes the key

3. Cookies

We use cookies for three specific purposes, which you can control via our cookie consent banner:

Essential cookies: Anonymous usage counter (rate limiting — no personal data). Always active.
Analytics cookies: Google Analytics — anonymous page views and country. Only with your consent.
Advertising cookies: Google AdSense — keeps the platform free. Only with your consent.

You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing cookies in your browser settings.
